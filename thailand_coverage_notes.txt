IMPLICATIONS FOR RESEARCH
-------------------------
Thailand now has 16 price rows from 7 Bangkok establishments. This is below
the threshold for inclusion in the UIFPI index aggregation (we require at
least 30 observations across at least 5 restaurants and 3 months per country
for the monthly index step). Therefore Thailand:
  - Appears in the prices table.
  - Is flagged in coverage_note when index_builder.py runs.
  - Is excluded from headline Granger results but reported descriptively
    in Section 5.2 of the paper as a coverage limitation.
